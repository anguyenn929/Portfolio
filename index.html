About Me



I am a student attending California State University, Long Beach working towards a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I have experience in programs such as CATIA, Solidworks, and MATLAB. My passions are aimed towards the physical (hands-on) and mechanical side of engineering, which include the automotive industry and CAD (computer-aided design).

I have always been interested in producing; the concept of creating to advance humankind. The ideas of design, production, and manufacturing have all been an inspiration. It has influenced me to become curious about the world and its future, resulting in my interests for automobiles, animation, and all sorts of modern advancements that benefit society.
